Know Budget Flights

Budget Flights is an European airline company incorporated under European Union law with its headquarters at Charles de Gaulle Airport in Tremblay-en-France, near Paris.It was established in 1st May 2018 with an objective of providing air facility to the people of Europe at low cost all over the Europe. The company has branch offices almost in every countries of Europe. Budget Flights is initially established to provide air transportation facility and hoping of developing and expanding the company in order to provide hotel booking as well as car rental facility in the days to come.The airlines name is made up of two words budget & flights which means inexpensive air transportation facility.

History

On 1 st May 2018, Budget flights was created by the mutual agreement between the member countries of the European Union and the Great Britain. As a result of the meeting, the French government take initiative for the establishment of the airlines company through the investment of the private companies and the government of all the countries of the Europe. Its market was subsequently increased to 25% compared to the other airlines company on the first few months. At the time of the festival in 2018, it provide free of cost flight to all the physically challenged people and infants. Upto now it has expanded its facility to every European countries. In the year 2018, it transported around 1.2 million passengers all over the Europe.
