Mobile app and site

To enhance your online experience, we have developed an easy-to-use mobile app and responsive website exclusively for Privilege Club members.

The app comes as an add-on feature to the Budget Flights app, giving you complete control over your membership account from your mobile device. It also provides you with a convenient and safe way to access your membership account on the go, anytime, anywhere.

With the app you can:

  • Access your dashboard and check your account details, including latest activities and upcoming trips, and generate statements for a specified period.
  • Use the Qcalculator to calculate Qmiles and Qpoints to be earned, as well as Qmiles required for redemption on Budget Flights, oneworld airlines.
  • Claim missing Qmiles for past flights.
  • Stay up to date with our latest offers and register for them.
  • Communicate with our Member Service Centre, for quicker processing of your requests.
  • Update your profile and communication preferences for email and SMS alerts from Budget Flights.
  • Access the Privilege Club mobile website, where you can learn more about the programme.
  • You can make your reservation later by saving your flight. You can finish the payment whenever you want on all of your devices.
  • From My Reservations section, you can add a new flight, you can change or cancel your flight and add infants to your flight.
  • Among various payment methods and currencies available on our mobile application, you can select the most convenient ones and make payments easily.
  • You can buy tickets a lot faster by saving your credit card or scanning your card with the camera at the payment stage.
  • You can always feel safe by hiding your credit card and profile information.
